These are the guidelines for your renovation project

Re-roofing

  • Replace missing or damaged sidingwith in-kind materials.
  • Repair or replace rotted sills and caulk around window and door frames.
  • Replace missing balustrades and rotted wood decking

Masonry and Stucco

  • Remove stucco from masonry surfaces where historically appropriate.
  • Avoid painting historically unpainted surfaces. 
  • Repoint masonry with compatible mortar and matching joint profile and composition

Foundations

  • Concrete piers are a suitable replacement for cedar when original piers are deteriorated beyond repair.
  • Many limestone foundations can be restored rather than replaced.
